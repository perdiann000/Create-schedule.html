The Golden Age of Cartoons?

Uncle Grandpa is a cartoon created by Peter Browngardt, which is one of my favorite shows of all time. But unfortunately, not a lot of people bother to watch it, mostly because they find it too weird compared to other animated programs. But the thing is, that's what its intending to, to BE weird. It has that style of old wacky 90's cartoons like Cow and Chicken and Ren and Stimpy.

Just remember, you can't judge a book by its over, even if you think it looks weird on the outside. It's what the thing behind it counts.

I figured that because not too many people are familiar with the show, or at least not know too much about it, I decided to make a website for it, since I have plenty of knowledge on it. And guess what, remember when I said it had that 90's style? Well this project's perfect for it!

Iconic 90's Cartoons

Uncle Grandpa

Uncle Grandpa is the main protoganist of the show, hence why the show is named after him. He goes around helping kids with their problems. But he helps them by taking them to crazy adventures instead of doing the easier way. However he also teaches them lessons throughout the journey to overcome their problems.

Mr. Gus

Mr. Gus is a lizard man bodyguard who protects Uncle Grandpa when tagging along his adventures. Unlike his other friends, he's a calm and serious of the group.

Pizza Steve

Pizza Steve is a sentient talking pizza who believes he's the coolest guy in the world. He also refers to himself in third person. Despite the fact that he's full of himself, he'd feel lost without his friends.

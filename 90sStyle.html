The Golden Age of Cartoons?

Most of us definitely watched cartoons as a kid, but there's still a vast majority of older people who still watch them. Just because they're animated doesn't mean older people can't enjoy them. But I'd say the 90's was a real golden age of cartoons. Sorry Disney, I love your movies and shows but to me they lack something what older cartoons (Mostly Cartoon Network) shows had.

Iconic 90's Cartoons

Spongebob Squarepants

Who lives in a pineapple under the sea? It's everyone's favorite sponge! Don't deny it, he was a big part of your childhood and to this very day, he's still trending. Especially in memes.

Mr. Gus

Mr. Gus is a lizard man bodyguard who protects Uncle Grandpa when tagging along his adventures. Unlike his other friends, he's a calm and serious of the group.

Pizza Steve

Pizza Steve is a sentient talking pizza who believes he's the coolest guy in the world. He also refers to himself in third person. Despite the fact that he's full of himself, he'd feel lost without his friends.
